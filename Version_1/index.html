Major Segments and Use Cases

A selection of use cases and the potential benefits blockchain may offer.

Industrial Manufacturing

Blockchain make the automation of remote system management possible. This combination of software, sensors and the network results in an increase of system efficiency and improve cost monitoring.

Contract Validation

Distribute ledgers enable the coding of contracts that will execute when specified conditions are met. For istance, a supply contract could be paid out automatically when agreed term and conditions are meet.

Payments

Blockchain can be used to give access to financial services to billions of people around the world, including those who don't have access to traditional banking. In addition banks can adopt this thecnology to make their business operations faster, more efficient and secure.

Product Auditing

Could revolutionize drug safety by enhancing counterfeit product surveillance, proactively detect counterfeit risk charachteristics and act as post-market surveillance shared ledger.

Identity Management

Availability of enhanced methods for verify identity in financial transactions online, e-commerce and for the sharing economy in general. Blockchain offers the additional possibility to digitize personal documents in a secure manner.

Personal Data

With blockchain users will have the ability to manage and sell the data their online activity generates. Because it can be easily distributed in small fractional amounts, some form of cryptocurrency will moost likely used for this type of transaction.
